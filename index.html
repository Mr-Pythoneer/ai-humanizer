Things this tool can't do for you

Automated rewriting only gets you so far — these matter just as much and require a human.

  • Add concrete, sensory detail. Swap vague abstractions for specific brand names, sounds, smells, and small real moments — a machine can't guess your specific memories.
  • Don't run this output through a grammar tool's "rewrite" button. Spell-check is fine; "Rewrite for Clarity" / "Make Professional" will iron your voice back into low-perplexity machine text.
  • Write the real draft in Google Docs or Word with version history on. A timestamped edit log — typos, deletions, pauses — is your best proof a human wrote it if anyone ever asks.
